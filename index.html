Global Gaming Syndicate

Made for Gamers, by Gamers.

What is GGS?

The main idea behind GGS is to be a place where players can come play in a competitive environment, where respect and improvement of others are the most valued traits.

Why join us?

GGS gives amateur players a chance to play in high-level tournaments so they can improve their skills and prove their worth. You never know, you might be a secret champion!

What do we offer?

GGS offers regular leagues and seasonal tournaments in which you will recieve full, detailed analysis and professionally casted matches on our Twitch channel.
